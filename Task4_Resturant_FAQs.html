What type of cuisine does Urban Spice offer?

Urban Spice offers a diverse range of cuisines, including Mughlai, Chinese, Pizza, BBQ & Grill, Indian, and a variety of desserts. We have something to satisfy every palate.

Reservations are recommended, especially during peak hours, to ensure you have a table waiting for you. You can make a reservation through our website or by calling us directly.
