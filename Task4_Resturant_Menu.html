Our Menu

Welcome to Urban Spice, where flavors come alive! Explore our diverse menu, ranging from rich Mughlai delicacies to mouthwatering biryanis, delectable pizzas, and a wide array of Indian culinary delights.

Biryani Paradise

Every grain tells a story. Dive into the world of biryanis, where fragrant rice and succulent meats unite for an irresistible feast.

Mughlai Delights

Indulge in Royalty: The Mughlai way! Our Mughlai dishes are a symphony of spices and flavors, offering a taste of culinary opulence.

Pizza Extravaganza

From Italy with Love: Our pizzas are made with a dash of urban flair and a pinch of gourmet perfection.

Indian Flavors

Embark on a culinary journey across India, where regional specialties come to life on your plate.

Chinese Delights

Savor the exotic taste of Chinese cuisine. From savory stir-fries to delectable dumplings, our Chinese dishes will transport your taste buds.

Vegetarian Varieties

Our vegetarian offerings are a paradise for herbivores, featuring an array of dishes rich in flavor and creativity.

Grill and BBQ

Experience the sizzle and aroma of our grill and BBQ section, where perfectly charred flavors meet tender, succulent meats.

Desserts

Sweet Endings: Experience a delightful finish to your meal with our dessert creations.
